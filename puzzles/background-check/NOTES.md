# Background Check

This 21×21 Sunday-style puzzle has 130 words, 80 blocks, rotational symmetry,
and all-over interlock.

Six starred phrases end with the names of fictional agents:

- `SCAVENGER HUNT` — Ethan Hunt of *Mission: Impossible*
- `MIDDLE ENGLISH` — Johnny English
- `COVALENT BOND` — James Bond
- `AMERICAN WEST` — Jim West of *The Wild Wild West*
- `KOSHER SALT` — Evelyn Salt of *Salt*
- `FLYING SOLO` — Napoleon Solo of *The Man from U.N.C.L.E.*

The revealer, `BACKGROUND AGENT`, puts those names at the backs of the starred
answers. It also refers to Cursor's remote coding feature. Cursor now calls the
product Cloud Agents and notes that it was formerly named Background Agents:

- [Cursor Cloud Agents documentation](https://cursor.com/docs/cloud-agent)

The original construction set also included `WICKED SMART` and
`SUPERPOWERS`. Those paired 11-letter entries were removed after repeated
21×21 fill attempts forced unsalvageable crossings. The retained six-agent set
produced a cleaner 130-word grid while preserving the revealer and title.

The canonical source is `puzzle.json`; `construction.json` records the final
theme placements and boundary blocks. Construction candidates and local fill
experiments remain under ignored `scratch/`.
