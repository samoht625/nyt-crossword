# Autocomplete

This 15×15 Monday-style puzzle uses `TRAINING`, `AGENT`, and `PROMPT` at the
starts of three long answers. The revealer, `GENERATIVE AI`, connects those
terms and identifies the technology used to help make the puzzle.

The canonical source was reconstructed from the original numbered blank grid
and clue PDF. The block pattern was transcribed from the grid, clue answers
were solved through their crossings, and the result was checked by the
repository validator.
