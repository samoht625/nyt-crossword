# AI Generated

This 15×15 Monday-style puzzle has 76 words, 42 blocks, rotational symmetry,
all-over interlock, and four theme entries.

The theme adds `A` before the standalone pronoun `I`, literally generating
`AI`:

- `WHAT AM I MISSING?` → `WHAT AM AI MISSING?`
- `DARE I SAY IT?` → `DARE AI SAY IT?`
- `I HATE TO SAY THIS` → `AI HATE TO SAY THIS`
- `AI GENERATED` is the revealer and describes the puzzle's origin.

The theme, grid, fill, clues, validation, and original submission materials
were developed with substantial generative-AI assistance and reviewed by
Thomas Carriero.

Fact-check notes for less common fill remain in `puzzle.json`.
